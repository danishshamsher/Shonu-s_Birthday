Our Story So Far

every little chapter, kept safe

26th November 2024

It all began at our department entry freshers' party on the 25th. 🌸 You looked breathtaking in your pink saree, and there I was in my grey blazer with a black shirt and pants, completely unaware that you already had the sweetest little crush on me. 🥹🤍
The next day, on the 26th, you quietly added that photo of just the two of us—your arm wrapped around mine—to your Close Friends story, with Yellow by Coldplay playing in the background. 💛🎶
To me, it was just a picture. To you, it was already a memory worth keeping. ✨
Looking back now, I realize that wasn't just an Instagram story—it was the first page of our love story, written by your heart long before mine caught up. 📖❤️
If I could visit that version of myself for just a moment, I'd whisper, "Pay attention... the girl in the pink saree is already falling for you, and she's about to become your whole world." 🌼

5th April 2025

Concert night at Invento. ✨ Our college ground was loud, alive, and overflowing with music, but my favorite memory isn't the stage—it's you. You were the one who pulled me in for a picture before we drifted apart, you to the girls' section and me to the boys'. 🤍

And then Raabta began to play. Somehow, even from opposite sides of the crowd, all I wanted was to be standing next to you. Turns out, you felt the exact same way. 🥹❤️

The moment the song ended, we called each other without a second thought, as if our hearts had already found their way back together before we did. From that night on, Raabta stopped being just another song—it quietly became ours. 🎶✨

Now, every time we sing it together, I'm taken back to that college ground, to the night we realized that no matter how big the crowd was, we'd always be looking for each other first. 💛

12th October 2025

Three words. They didn't surprise me—not because they meant any less, but because we'd both known, long before that midnight text, what our hearts had been quietly saying. 🤍 At exactly 12:17 a.m., your "I love you" on WhatsApp simply gave a name to a feeling we'd already been living. ✨

I was scared—not of loving you, but of my past, and of the future we knew might never be ours because of our castes. Even then, we chose each other. We chose to love fully for as long as life would let us, believing that if one day we had to part, we'd leave with the most beautiful memories, carrying a piece of each other for the rest of our lives. 🥹❤️

You never asked me to say it back immediately. You just smiled and told me to say it when I felt safe, when it came from my whole heart. That kind of love is rare—it never rushed, never demanded, it simply waited. 🌼

And when I finally said, "I love you," it wasn't just a reply. It was my heart finding its way home to the girl who loved me bravely first. ♾️💛

18th November 2025

It wasn't the perfect day I'd imagined. Nothing really went according to plan, and almost everything I had hoped for fell apart. But maybe love was never waiting for the perfect moment anyway. 🤍

After weeks of carrying those words in my heart, I finally looked at you and said, "I love you." Not because everything was perfect, but because my feelings were. 🥹❤️

The day may not be my favorite memory, but the confession always will be. Because no matter how messy the moment was, the truth I gave you was the most certain thing I'd ever known. ✨

Looking back now, I don't remember the plans that failed. I only remember finally telling the girl who had loved me so patiently that I loved her too. And somehow, that was enough. 🌼♾️

5th January 2026

Some moments don't just change your story—they remind you how precious it is to still have one. 🤍 Just a few days earlier, on the 30th of December, I had a terrifying accident that came far too close to taking everything away. Life suddenly felt fragile in a way I'd never known.

Then came the 5th of January. You came to see me, and for the first time since the accident, everything felt okay again. Somewhere between our conversations, you pulled me closer with a soft "shii kissy," and in the next heartbeat, we shared our very first kiss. 🥹❤️

It wasn't dramatic or perfectly planned. It was gentle, quiet, and exactly what my heart needed. In that one moment, the fear I'd been carrying melted away, replaced by nothing but gratitude—that I was still here, that you were beside me, and that I got to experience something so beautiful with the person I loved. ✨

I'll never forget that day. Not just because it was our first kiss, but because it reminded me that after coming so close to losing life, I had been given another chance to live it... and to kiss the girl who made me happiest to be alive. ♾️💛

& many more chapters to come...
