Our Story So Far

every little chapter, kept safe

26th November 2024

It all began at our department entry freshers' party on the 25th. 🌸 You looked breathtaking in your pink saree, and there I was in my grey blazer with a black shirt and pants, completely unaware that you already had the sweetest little crush on me. 🥹🤍
The next day, on the 26th, you quietly added that photo of just the two of us—your arm wrapped around mine—to your Close Friends story, with Yellow by Coldplay playing in the background. 💛🎶
To me, it was just a picture. To you, it was already a memory worth keeping. ✨
Looking back now, I realize that wasn't just an Instagram story—it was the first page of our love story, written by your heart long before mine caught up. 📖❤️
If I could visit that version of myself for just a moment, I'd whisper, "Pay attention... the girl in the pink saree is already falling for you, and she's about to become your whole world." 🌼

5 April 2025

Concert night. The kind of loud, electric night that blurs into flashing lights and a thousand strangers singing the same words. And somewhere in the middle of all that noise, you and I found this one hushed pocket of stillness that was only ours. When "Raabta" played, something shifted — the crowd seemed to fade, the noise dimmed, and there was just Arijit Singh's voice threading through the small space between us. I don't remember exactly when it happened, only that by the time the song ended, it had already claimed itself as ours, the way songs sometimes do without asking permission. Every time it plays now, I'm thrown right back into those lights and that crowd, and the exact way it felt to realize I wanted you beside me at every loud, beautiful night for the rest of my life.

12 October 2025

Three words. That's all it took to rearrange everything I thought I knew about how careful I was supposed to be with my heart. You said it first — quietly, bravely, like you'd been carrying it around for a while and finally set it down in front of me. I remember the exact stillness of that moment, how the whole world seemed to pause just long enough for it to sink in. I think about how much courage that takes, to hand someone your feelings before you know for certain they'll be caught. You caught me completely off guard, and in doing so, you caught me — full stop. That day didn't just change the way I saw us; it changed the way I understood love itself, as something worth being brave for.

18 November 2025

It took me a little longer to find the words — not because I felt any less, but because saying them out loud made everything feel so real. So when I finally said it back, it wasn't sudden or impulsive, it was the truest, most deliberate thing I'd ever told you. I'd spent weeks turning the words over in my head, testing whether they were big enough to hold what I actually felt, and I don't think they ever fully were. But I said them anyway, because you deserved to hear them, and because I was done being careful with something that already belonged to you. I still remember the way your face changed when I said it, like a held breath finally let go. From that night on, "I love you" stopped being three words and started being a promise I intend to keep saying, in a hundred different ways, for as long as you'll let me.

5 January 2026

Some moments announce themselves loudly, and some just arrive quietly, exactly when they're supposed to. Ours was the second kind — no grand buildup, no perfectly staged scene, just the two of us, finally close enough that neither of us was pretending we didn't want it. I remember being nervous in a way I hadn't been in years, and I remember that nervousness disappearing the second it actually happened. It felt less like a first and more like something we'd both been quietly waiting to arrive at, patient underneath all the timing. Every kiss since has carried a little bit of that same feeling — new every time, but rooted in that one quiet moment we finally let ourselves have. 5th January will always be the day a maybe turned into an absolutely.

& many more chapters to come...
